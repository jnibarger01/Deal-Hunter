## 7) FAQ (8 Q/A)

### 1) What is Deal-Hunter?
Deal-Hunter is a deal discovery and valuation platform for resellers. It ingests listings, analyzes sold comps, estimates True Market Value, and ranks opportunities by profit and risk.

### 2) Which marketplaces are supported?
Deal-Hunter is currently built around eBay data in v1, with architecture designed to expand to additional sources in future phases.

### 3) How is TMV different from a simple average price?
TMV applies outlier filtering and recency weighting, then adds a confidence score based on sample quality. That gives you a more dependable value estimate than a raw average.

### 4) Can Deal-Hunter guarantee profit?
No tool can guarantee profit. Deal-Hunter provides decision support so you can evaluate opportunities faster and with better data.

### 5) Who is this for?
Resellers and flippers who want to source inventory with a repeatable, data-first process — from part-time side hustlers to full-time operators.

### 6) Do I need technical skills to use it?
No. The platform is built for fast decision-making with a straightforward dashboard, filters, and ranked opportunities.

### 7) What signals are included in deal analysis?
Depending on available data, Deal-Hunter evaluates price spread, confidence, volatility, liquidity, and estimated time-to-sell to help you judge deal quality.

### 8) How quickly does data update?
Data refresh cadence depends on source and sync settings, but the system is designed for near-real-time monitoring so you can act before opportunities vanish.
